6 Honey Face Packs to Treat Skin Issues.

  1. Honey + Nutmeg to Clear Acne: Nutmeg contains essential oils that are beneficial for your skin. When combined with honey, the antiseptic and antimicrobial properties add up to fight pimples and acne without irritating or drying out the skin. This face pack brightens skin and deeply cleanses out pores as well.
  2. Honey + Aloe vera + Lemon Juice: To even out skin tone and to lighten dark spots, mix aloe vera and honey in equal amount, and add 2 to 3 drops of lemon juice. Mix well, keep for half an hour and rinse off.
  3. Honey + Turmeric + Aloe Vera = Just add a pinch of turmeric in equal amounts of aloe vera and honey, and apply all over the face and neck to brighten up dull skin and to remove tan as well.
  4. Honey + Milk: The lactic acid content of milk and the moisturizing properties of honey combine together to brighten up dull skin and rejuvenate the complexion. Use both these amazing ingredients in equal quantities.
  5. Raw Honey + Olive Oil = This is an amazing face pack for dry skin. Take 1 tbsp of raw honey and mix 1 tbsp of olive oil. Massage all over face and rinse with lukewarm water after 15 minutes. People with oily skin can replace olive oil with jojoba oil which is non-comedogenic in nature.
  6. Honey + Rice Flour + Tea Tree Oil = To clear out blackheads, take 1 tbsp rice flour, add 1 tbsp honey and 2 drops of tea tree oil. Mix well, apply over the blackheads, and rinse off with lukewarm water after 20 minutes.

15 Ways To Use Turmeric for Removing Pimples

  1. Turmeric and Honey Mask:
    This is another easy-to-use face pack for pimples. You just need 1 tsp of turmeric and 2 tsp of honey. Turmeric and honey both have strong anti-microbial properties and fight pimples together. Honey also helps in healing acne and reducing inflammation.
  2. Turmeric and Mint Juice Mask:
    Mint is a great ingredient to use in face pack along with turmeric. Mint brightens complexion, calms and soothes inflammation and pimples, and hydrates and tones skin. To make this mask, add 2 tsp of fresh mint juice with 1 tsp of turmeric. Add a bit of honey if you want. Apply this mask on skin and keep it for 15 minutes. Rinse off and pat dry your skin. Do this thrice a week.
  3. Turmeric, Egg White, Honey and Lemon Mask:
    Egg whites have an enzyme called lysozyme that fights acne-causing microbes. Turmeric, as you all know, is a strong antioxidant. Egg white also helps to tighten and firm skin. So, this is a good anti-ageing mask as well. To make this face pack, combine 1 tsp of turmeric, one full egg white, 1 tsp of honey, and just a small squeeze of lemon. Mix well and apply on the skin for 15 minutes. Do this three times a week.
  4. Turmeric, Orange Powder, and Honey:
    This is a face brightening and lightening pack as well. Orange peel powder has high vitamin C content and along with turmeric and honey, it will not only get rid of acne, it will clear up acne scars too with regular use.
